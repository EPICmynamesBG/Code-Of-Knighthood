	The Code of Chivalry not a specific set of rules that were written down for knights to follow. They are a set of moral ideals that went beyond the rules of combat. These morals included:
	>> Fair Play- exhibit self-discipline, provide justice and mercy
	>> Valor- courage in word and action, defend the weak and avenge the wronged
	>> Honor- keep one’s word and principles
	>> Courtesy- exhibit manners, be respectful of host, authority and women
	>> Loyalty- be faithful to God, the King, and the code, be true to one’s beloved
	>> Largess- be generous, show hospitality to strangers
	>> Piety- be well grounded in one’s faith, depend upon God
